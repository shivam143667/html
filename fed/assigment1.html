My Favorite Book: "The Great Gatsby"

"The Great Gatsby" is a novel written by F. Scott Fitzgerald and was first published in1925 . It is widely considered one of the greatest American novels. The story is set during the Roaring Twentiesand explores themes of love, wealth, and American dream.

The novel's main character, Jay Gatsby, is famous for his extravagant parties and unrequited love for Daisy Buchanan. his story ultimateld leads to tragic consequences. The book is known for its symbolismand its critical view of the american dream.
